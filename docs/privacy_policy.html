Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 7, 2025

This Privacy Policy outlines how the C-Cell App, developed for the Career and Guidance Cell at LNMIIT, collects, uses, and protects user information. By using the app, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Your name, email address, and institute roll number when you sign in through Google or other means.
  • Device Information: Device type, OS version, and app usage data may be collected anonymously to improve the user experience.
  • Notification Data: Notifications you receive, read, or interact with may be logged for analytics purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To display personalized and relevant notifications or event details.
  • To improve app functionality and reliability.
  • To analyze aggregated usage patterns for better student engagement.

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data. Your information may only be accessed by authorized members of the C-Cell for internal use.

4. Data Security

We take appropriate measures to secure your data using encrypted transmission, Firebase Authentication, and secure backend APIs. However, no system can be completely secure, so use the app at your own discretion.

5. Third-Party Services

We use services such as Firebase (Authentication and Firestore), Google Sign-In, and analytics tools, which may collect information in accordance with their own privacy policies.

6. User Rights

You may request deletion of your data by contacting us. To withdraw access or remove your account, please reach out via the contact email provided below.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted here and will take effect immediately upon being published.

8. Contact Us
